## ‚ú® Key Features

- **Disease Metamorphosis**  
  Step-by-step understanding of how mental symptoms evolve into physical pathology.

- **Disease Nature & Miasm**  
  Track the natural course of disease and underlying miasmatic tendencies.

- **Repertory & Materia Medica**  
  Search and filter through classical repertories and materia medica

- **Medicine Snapshot**  
  Quick, high-yield overviews of each remedy ‚Äì essence, keynotes, modalities, relationships.

- **Diagnostic Test Interpretation**  
  Homeopathic perspective on common lab reports and investigations.

- **Causation-Based Remedy Selection**  
  Focus on etiology (grief, shock, suppression, exposure, etc.) to refine remedy choice.

- **Case Analysis & Repertorization**  
  - Rubric search and selection  
  - Remedy ranking  
  - Export/share analysis as PDF/Image for discussion with colleagues or students.
