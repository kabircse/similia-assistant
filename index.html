Core clinical features

Similia Assistant is built to mirror the way classical homeopaths actually think through cases – from disease evolution to repertorization and remedy selection.

Disease metamorphosis Miasmatic view Repertory search Materia medica Drug relationships Case analysis
  • Disease Metamorphosis: Mental থেকে physical – রোগের স্বাভাবিক রূপান্তরকে ধাপে ধাপে visual ভাবে বোঝা।
  • Disease Nature & Miasm: Psora, Sycosis, Syphilis ইত্যাদির long-term প্রভাব মাথায় রেখে কেস প্ল্যান করা।
  • Repertory & MM: Rubric search, remedy grades, keynotes, modalities – সব এক জায়গায়।
  • Remedy Snapshot: দ্রুত স্মরণের জন্য essence, core mental state, generals ও key physicals।
  • Diagnostic Test hints: Common lab reports–কে case context-এ বুঝতে সাহায্যকারী নোট (not a lab substitute).
Decision support for serious practice

How Similia helps

Similia Assistant is meant to support, not replace, a skilled homeopathic physician. It focuses on clarity of thinking rather than automation of prescriptions.

  • Clear rubric selection and symptom hierarchy (mental > general > local).
  • Remedy ranking with space for your own clinical judgment.
  • Case notes, snapshots and exports for study, supervision or peer discussion.
Important: Similia Assistant does not provide medical diagnosis or emergency treatment. It is an educational and professional decision-support tool for trained homeopaths and students under supervision.
