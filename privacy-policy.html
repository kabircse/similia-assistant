Privacy Policy – Similia Assistant

Last updated: 2025-01-01

1. Introduction

Similia Assistant (“we”, “our”, “the App”) is a digital homeopathy companion designed for homeopathic doctors, students, and researchers. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the App and related services.

By using Similia Assistant, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

The exact data collected may depend on which features of the App you use (e.g., offline only, or with online services / cloud sync). In general, we may collect the following categories of information:

2.1 Device and technical information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version and basic technical logs
  • Anonymous crash reports and performance metrics

2.2 Usage data

  • Screens or sections of the App that you access
  • Buttons or features you interact with
  • Approximate time spent in the App

2.3 Optional user data (if account / cloud features are enabled)

If the App includes login, sync, or cloud backup features, we may collect:

  • Name or display name
  • Email address
  • Encrypted authentication tokens
  • Case records, notes, or preferences that you explicitly choose to store in the cloud

If you use the App completely offline without any account features, we may not collect any personally identifiable information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide and maintain the core functionality of the App
  • Improve stability, performance, and user experience
  • Identify and fix bugs or crashes
  • (If cloud features exist) synchronize your data securely across devices that you authorize

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We also do not use your data for unrelated advertising without your knowledge.

4. Data Storage and Security

Similia Assistant may store data in two main ways:

  • Locally on your device: Case data, notes and preferences may be saved on the device’s local storage or database.
  • In the cloud: If you use online features (such as sync or backup), data may be stored on a secure backend service (for example, a provider such as Supabase or another reputable cloud platform).

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, and you use the App at your own risk.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for functionality such as:

  • Crash reporting and analytics
  • Authentication and backend data storage
  • Optional messaging or email delivery

These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies. We only use reputable services and aim to minimize the personal data shared with them.

6. Children’s Privacy

Similia Assistant is primarily intended for use by adult homeopathic practitioners and students under appropriate supervision. It is not designed for unsupervised use by children under 13 years of age.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.
