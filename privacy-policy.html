Privacy Policy Overview

Similia Assistant collects only the data needed to keep the app secure, private, and functional for homeopathic practice support. The sections below explain what is collected, why it is collected, how it stays encrypted, and which controls you have on your device.

Data we collect

  • FCM token: For Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver announcements and reminders.
  • Crash reports: Firebase Crashlytics receives anonymized traces to help debug stability issues.
  • Device identifiers: Used to derive the SQLCipher encryption key so the bundled database stays tied to each install.
  • Local preferences: Settings such as dark mode, font scale, and saved choices are stored only on the device.

Why we collect it

  • Messaging: Firebase Messaging needs the token to route notifications to your registered device.
  • Stability: Crashlytics informs us of crashes so we can keep fixing bugs.
  • Encryption: Device-bound keys ensure the embedded database cannot be restored on another device without the original install.
  • Experience: Local preferences keep the app usable offline without syncing any profile information.

Storage, sharing & retention

Data stays encrypted on the Android device by default (SQLCipher + AES). Only the FCM token and anonymized crash telemetry leave the device through Firebase.

Firebase (Google) is the only third party involved; we do not share or sell personal information. Uninstalling the app removes the encrypted database and all settings by design.

Your control

  • Disable or adjust notifications via Android's app settings if you want to stop FCM usage.
  • Reinstall the app to force a fresh database rebuild whenever you suspect corruption.
  • Contact kabir.cse10@gmail.com with any data-handling questions.
  • Firebase's privacy guarantees apply; no personal data leaves the device beyond crash and notification telemetry.
This policy keeps personal and sensitive data explicit for Google Play and transparency to users.
